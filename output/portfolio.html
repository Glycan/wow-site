At WOW! Balloons we do balloon decorations! From small birthday parties to elegant wedding decorations to trade shows and conventions!

Of course we do balloon arches , balloon bouquets and balloon centerpieces ! We also do much MUCH more! Most of our balloon decorations are of the unusual, creative and never-seen-before variety. Balloon sculptures and balloon walls , lighting and special effects, balloon decorations that stay in one place and balloon decorations that move, balloon drops, popping balloons and explosive balloon walls.

Just call us and we’ll make it happen. For the last 18 years, our highly-talented staff has designed outstanding displays for Coca-Cola and local schools, AT&T and fund raisers, grand opening of the MCI Arena and the Sysco tradeshow, and many other clients.

Yes, our main material is a balloon. This is the brick we use to make castles, to create sculptures. So, just as an architect offers not bricks, but buildings, we offer balloon décor. Whether you're planning an elegant wedding or a giant corporate party, the spectacular displays from WOW Balloons will make it an event to remember.
