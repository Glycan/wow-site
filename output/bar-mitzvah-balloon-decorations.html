Bar Mitzvah - Bat Mitzvah Balloon Decorations

Is there anything more important to a young man or woman than the ritual of coming of age, of being accepted as a fully fledged member of their culture and religion? There is only one such moment in the life of your son or daughter, so make it as memorable as possible.

Thus the significance of the Bar Mitzvah party and Bat Mitzvah party, when family and friends gather together to say, “You may now participate fully. You have matured into an amazing person, an adult who is accountable and responsible in the eyes of your elders and we now formalize and celebrate your arrival with the time honored tradition of the Mitzvah.”
